What Is {{ settings.SITE_NAME }}?

{{ settings.SITE_NAME }} is a service that provides you with an infinite number of unique inboxes. It's not designed to replace your personal email address, but rather complement it. Every time a website asks for your email, click the "Add Inbox" button to create another email address. This leaves your personal address free from promotional offers and other junk mail.
